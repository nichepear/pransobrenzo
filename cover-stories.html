Best mortadella fastfood in town

12 june 2022
street food

Even though there are varieties of Mortadella across Italy, the best-known version is Mortadella from Bologna.

If you have never tried Mortadella, you should try the fresh cuts! You can get it directly from salumeria like Simoni, Ceccarelli, or Baita

You can also find cold cuts in many restaurants or aperitivo bars.

Apart from the cold cut platter, another widespread Mortadella consumption is a part of the street food menu, like panini, piadina, tigelle, and crescentina.

Our top of mind goes to "Indegno - la crescentina 2.0", where they serve Mortadella with tasty fried crescentina! After you have tried this, you can say you've truly arrived in Bologna.

Then there is a nice tigelle stuffed with Mortadella - "Tigellino" where you can get the tigelle with various stuff from fresh cheese to Nutella at a perfect price.

Our interns' favourite goes to Mortadella Lab , where you can get creative panini menus with Mortadella, just steps away from via Pratello, where young folks usually hang out.
